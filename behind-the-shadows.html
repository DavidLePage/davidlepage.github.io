Behind the shadows

June 14, 2010

[gallery]

This afternoon, in Obs, I attended a rehearsal of shadow puppetry led by Unima CEO and Standard Bank Young Artist for Theatre 2010 Janni Younge. Unima is the International Union of Puppetry South Africa. It was a freezing afternoon, and a good time to get lost in light and shade.
